Quiche Recipe

This is a recipe to make the best quiche you'll ever have in your life

First of all we are going the inside of the quiche then add it together and cook it

    Ingredients:
  • puff pastry sheet
  • milk and cream
  • bacon
  • egg
  • Salt & Pepper
    Process to follow (Follow each step in order)
  1. cut bacon into pieces.
  2. break 3 eggs into a bowl
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Add cream
  5. mix everything.
  6. Put puff pastry into a pie dish and dip all the mix in
  7. Cook until golden brown and ready to serve
