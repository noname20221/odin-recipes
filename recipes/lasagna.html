Lasagna Recipe

This is a recipe to make the best lasagne you'll ever have in your life

First of all we are going to make the sauce then we are gonna see how to assemble and cook everything together to get that amazing dish

    Ingredients:
  • lasagna pasta
  • meat
  • Tomato products
  • omion & garlic
    Process to follow (Follow each step in order)
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
